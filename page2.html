ABOUT US

WE SAW A PROBLEM AND WANTED TO SOLVE IT

After talking with local lawn mowers and tradies, we noticed a few common problems starting to appear. Thats when Zeit was born, German for "time" we save time in peoples already busy lifes through a simple, yet efficient process from the finding of the service to the payment which happens in the background, without even being noticed. We change the way people think about finding, booking and paying for services, so they can spend time with the people that matter to them the most.
